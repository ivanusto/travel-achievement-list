# 🌍 Earth Travel Achievements

🌐 **Languages / 多語言版本**: [繁體中文](README.md) | [English](README.en.md)

A personal global travel footprint check-in and share card generator platform that combines an **achievement system**, **tile aesthetics**, and **notebook style**.

This system is fully deployed in the cloud. Players do not need to install any program or download files, and can directly complete all check-in records and travel achievement check-ins in the browser.

---

## 🌟 Core Features

1. **Dashboard Statistics**: Real-time calculation of player levels (1 level per 10 achievements) and progressive titles (e.g., `🌱 Travel Novice`, `✈ Adventure Explorer`, `🌌 Cosmic Travel Deity`, etc.).
2. **55 Countries/Regions Footprint Map**: A 15x10 abstract geographical grid map presenting popular travel destinations worldwide. Click to check in directly and stamp a red visit mark, dynamically showing progress by region.
3. **Canvas Progress Radar Chart**: Drawn with native Canvas, without any external third-party JS library dependencies, perfectly displaying exploration progress across 10 categories.
4. **268 Global Achievements Database**: Covers multiple difficulties from Common to Legendary, supporting **Steam-like hidden achievements** with surprise descriptions revealed upon unlocking.
5. **12 Honor Badges Wall**: Automatically determines unlock progress for badges like `🎒 Backpacker`, `🍜 World Gourmet`, `🏛️ Civilization Explorer`, `👑 World Domination`, and more.
6. **One-Click Share Card (PNG)**: Canvas automatically synthesizes an 800x1200 high-resolution player card containing your nickname, avatar, level, badge wall, and radar chart.
7. **Dual Themes**: Supports a dark Steam gaming theme and a light traditional Washi paper notebook theme.
8. **Backup and Restore**: Auto-saves to LocalStorage, with support for exporting and importing JSON progress files for easy cross-device synchronization.
9. **No Backend Required**: A completely static, single-page HTML file. It loads extremely fast and keeps 100% of user data stored locally on your device for privacy.
